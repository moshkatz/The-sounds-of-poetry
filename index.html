A little about me...

My name is Moshe Katz and I was born in Jerusalem, I'm 24 years old after military service at 8200.

From my childhood, I suffered from barely self-expression and would rarely talk and communicate with new friends. So I was only play with a small group of friends and in my spare time I would play on pc.

Over the years, I've taken refuge from the difficulties of guided imagination. I'd get myself into characters and tell myself a story that I'm his inventor

After high school, I went to yeshiva, where people shared stories and poems they wrote, There I was discovered my ability,
ability to put my thoughts songs and stories into the paper without wanting to anyone.

My first song I went up anonymity, because I didn't want to be judged by my ag, And that's where it touched someone.
The same guy it touched looked for me and thanked me for the words, It shook him and revived him.

It's a little taste of my songs and stories
